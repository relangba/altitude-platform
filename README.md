# Altitude Platform

> **Empowering Science, Conservation, and Community through Open Technology.**

Altitude Platform is an open-source initiative to develop a modern digital platform for science education, environmental conservation, and non-profit organizations.

The project begins as a responsive static website and is designed to evolve into a modular web application supporting citizen science, volunteer coordination, event management, educational resources, and organizational operations.

---

## Vision

To make science education, environmental conservation, and community engagement more accessible through open technology.

---

## Project Goals

- 🌿 Promote environmental awareness
- 🐦 Support birdwatching and citizen science
- 🔭 Encourage astronomy and STEM education
- 🤝 Organize volunteers and outreach programs
- 📅 Manage educational events and workshops
- 📚 Share scientific resources and learning materials
- 💻 Build an open-source platform for science organizations and NGOs

---

## Development Roadmap

### Phase 1

### Phase 4

- Certificate generation
